Final Deliverables

This visual essay explores how cookie consent systems frame unpaid digital labour as user convenience. Running at 1 minute 33 seconds, it combines stock footage sourced from Pexels with original narration recorded over a script developed throughout this module. Background music — Robotic Dreams by DSTechnician — was chosen to reinforce a sense of unease around unseen digital systems. The essay is aimed at general internet users, particularly younger audiences, and aims to inform rather than alarm. It was edited in Adobe Premiere Pro.
